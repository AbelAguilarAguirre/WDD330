Packages

Although we don't offer custom cakes, we have an extensive selection of delicious treats and packages to suit your needs!

Our packages come with a full setup, complete takedown, and thorough cleanup.

Please keep in mind that the delivery fee varies based on the location of your event. We provide our own table with a fabric backdrop, linens, dessert stands, bases, serving trays, glass jars, and table decorations that are tailored to your theme or occasion.

We can also reserve space on the table for your cake and offer a cake stand upon request.

Additionally, we offer add-ons for all packages.

If you have specific dessert requests or are hosting an event with more than 100 guests, please fill out the inquiry form below, and we'll schedule a phone consultation with you.

Planning a smaller gathering with less than 35 guests? Be sure to check out our à la carte menu!

All dessert cups are in 3 oz cups and mini spoons are provided.
